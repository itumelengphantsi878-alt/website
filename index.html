About Us

Green Horizons Youth Network is a community-focused non-profit organisation dedicated to advancing climate resilience through education and practical action. We empower youth and communities with the knowledge and tools to adapt to climate change, protect natural resources, and foster a sustainable future.

Through this initiative, we asked ourselves, “What can we do differently, and how can we make an impact?” That question became the heartbeat of our organization. Every step we take is driven by the belief that small actions, when combined, can create lasting change.

Our Programs

Climate Education & Awareness

We translate complex climate concepts into practical, locally relevant solutions through education, awareness campaigns, and participatory learning approaches.

School Outreach & Climate Clubs

We run interactive school sessions and establish climate clubs that empower learners to lead discussions, organise activities, and take environmental action.

Community Engagement

We host community workshops that connect climate knowledge to everyday experiences, encourage dialogue, and promote collective responsibility.

Our Impact

Youth & Community Reach

Since its inception, Green Horizons Youth Network has engaged over 500 youth and community members through climate education programs and outreach activities.

Community Workshops

We have conducted 12 community engagement workshops in local community spaces, fostering dialogue and practical climate action.

School Climate Clubs

We have established five climate school clubs, engaging approximately 100 learners in ongoing climate education and environmental action.

Measured Learning Outcomes

Pre- and post-engagement feedback shows that 70% of participants demonstrate improved climate knowledge and awareness.
